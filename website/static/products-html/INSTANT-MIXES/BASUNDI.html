Basundi Recipe: Heat 500 Ml Milk till Lukewarm.

Add Satvam Basundi Mix to it and stir well

Bring the milk to boil stirring continuously till it becomes slightly thicker.

Let it cool for some time and serve. Refrigerate for some time if you wish to make it chilled.
