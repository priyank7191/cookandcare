Dosa Recipe: Add 360 Ml of water to Satvam Dosa Mix. Mix well and make it a smooth batter. Keep it aside for 5 minutes.

Take a flat griddle (Tava) or non-stick pan. Smear some oil on it.

Pour 75 Ml batter in the center of the pan. Spread it in outward circular motion to make a 8 Inch diameter thin circle.

Heat the pan on a medium flame. Pour a few drops of oil over it and along the edges. Cook till it becomes golden brown. Fold it to a semi circle or a roll and serve hot with sambhar and coconut chutney.

For the next Dosa, sprinkle little water on pan and wipe the surface gently with a cloth. Repeat step 3 onwards for the remaining batter.

Important : Ensure that the pan is cool before you start making every dosa.
