Gota Mix: Add 340 ml of water to Satvam Gota Mix. Mix well to make a batter of dropping consistency. Keep aside for 15 mins.

Heat oil in a kadai. Stir the batter vigorously and put spoonfuls of batter in the oil and deep fry gota in oil at a consistent temperature. Drain on absorbent paper and serve hot with Satvam's Red Chutney.

Note: You can also make `Fulvadi' from this mixture.

Chutney Recipe: Add 100 gms of jaggery into 130 ml of water. Simmer till the jaggery has melted. Add Satvam Red Chutney powder in it and boil it for 3-5 mins. Cool the mixture and serve it with hot Satvam Gota.
