Handvo Recipe: Add 310 Ml water to Satvam Handvo Mix and stir well.

Keep the mixture aside for 15 minutes.

Mix well before pouring the mixture into the Handvo vessel/cooker.

'For Tadka': In a pan heat 60 ml of oil and temper some mustard seeds. Once the seeds start crackling, add sesame seeds, dried red chilli, asafetida and curry leaves in tempering, Pour the 'tadka' on Handvo Mix.

Cover the lid and let it cook for 20-25 minutes on a medium flame. If using a pan for cooking, flip the Handvo with a flat spoon after 10-15 minutes or till the base turns golden brown in colour. Cut the Handvo to required size and serve hot.
