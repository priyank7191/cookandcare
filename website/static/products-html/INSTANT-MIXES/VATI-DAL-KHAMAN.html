Vati Dal Khaman Recipe: Add Vati Dal Khaman Mix into a bowl with 240 ml of water and stir well. Keep the mixture aside for 10 - 15 minute.

Mix well and spread it on oil layered plate. Steam for 15 - 20 minute.

Remove the plates and let it cool for 10 - 15 minute.

For Tadka (Seasoning): Cut the Khaman into equal pieces and pour a seasoning (tadka) of 80 ml Oil (Preferably Groundnut Oil), 1/4 tsp Mustard Seeds, finely chopped Green Chillies and pinch of Asafoetida on it. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves, grated coconut & crushed black pepper. Serve with chutney.

Chutney Recipe: Add 95 - 100 ml of water to Chutney Powder. Stir well and make a mixture without lumps. Boil it for 5 minutes to make a thick paste. Serve hot with Vati Dal Khaman.
