Rava Idli Recipe: Add 290 ml water in Satvam Rava Idli Mix. Whip well to make it a smooth mixture.

Add chopped green chillies and chopped coriander leaves into the mixture and mix well and keep aside for 10 Mins.

Blend the mixture well before steaming.

Grease the Idli cooker or vessel. Pour the batter in the moulds. Tie the cloth tightly on the lid of the vessel and let the batter steam for about 15 Mins on full flame.

Remove the Idlis from the vessel and serve hot with Sambhar & coconut Chutney.
