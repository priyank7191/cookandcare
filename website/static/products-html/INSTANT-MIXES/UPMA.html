Upma Recipe: Boil 1.5 ltr water in thick bottom vessel.

Add contents of the pack and 5 tbsp oil/ghee and mix well.

Tip 1: You can also add boiled peas

Tip 2: For spicy Upma, add 2 - 3 chopped green chilli to the mix.

Simmer for 5 minutes stirring occasionally till the water is absorbed. Garnish with Coriander Leaves & serve hot.

Tip 3: Add 2.5 tbsp ghee / butter before serving.
