Idli Recipe: Add 310 ml water to Satvam Idli Mix. Blend it until it becomes smooth batter.

Keep it aside for 15 minutes. Blend the mixture well before steaming.

Grease the idli cooker or vessel. Pour the batter in the moulds. Tie a cloth tightly on the led of vessel and let the batter steam for about 15 minutes on full name.

Remove the Idlis from the vessel and serve hot with sambhar & coconut chutney.

Note : you can also make 'Idada' from this mix. To make Idada more delicious, sprinkle black pepper powder on it.
