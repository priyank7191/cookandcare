Dahiwada Recipe: Add 290 ml water to Satvam Dahiwada Mix & mix well to make it of dropping consistency.

Keep it aside for 15 Minutes.

Stir the batter again & deep fry wadas in Oil at a consistent temperature.

Take water in a deep bowl and soak wadas in it for 5 minutes. Flatten & press each wada lightly between your palms to remove excess water.

Serve wadas topped with half-sweetened curd (approx. 400 gms). Sprinkle black chopped coriander leaves and tamarind-date chutney as per taste.
