Black Pepper Powder gives a warm spicy aroma to the dishes which is why it is extensively used in Indian cuisine as well as the world.

Black Pepper is one of the four most important ingredients of Garam Masala.

It has been used as folk medicine for centuries and it exerts some positive medicinal benefits on digestion system, psychology, respiratory system and immunity.
