Kesari Milk Masala Recipe: Add 1/2 tsp Satvam Kesari Milk Masala to 2 cup of Milk. Add sugar to taste and boil.

Serve hot or chilled.

Satvam Kesari Milk Masala is also used as natural taste and flavour enhancer to Ice Cream, Kulfi, Basundi, Kheer, Milk Shake, Sheer Khurma etc. and can be used to garnish Indian sweets.
