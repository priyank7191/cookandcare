Cut approx. 1 kg unripe mangos in to small pieces and mix the pieces with 200 gm Satvam Achar Masala.

Thoroughly mix them and put the mixture in the vessel.

Take 200 gm oil, heat it and then allow it to cool.

Pour the oil in the vessel containing the mango and achar masala mixture and set them aside.
