SWEET-MASALA

Take 5 kg peeled mango and cut them into small pieces. Mix appropriate amount of salt and and turmeric powder. Stir it twice or thrice a day.

After 24 hour, remove mango pieces from salt - turmeric and allow it to dry over cloth for 3 hour

Now, mix mango pieces to 5 kg jaggery and 1 Kg Satvam Sweet Achar Masala. Allow it to stand for five days. Stir the mixture twice a day

After five days, upon proper mixing of jagerry fill the mixture in cleaned jar. Add preheated cooled edible oil as per requirement. Sweet pickle of your taste is ready now.
